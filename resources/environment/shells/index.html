Shells

From my experience two shells that are worth notice are

  • ZSH
  • Fish

In practice though, you can achieve Fish features installing particular plugins for ZSH, and yet, ZSH will still be more compatible with Bash, while Fish introduces it’s own syntax and quirks, which may be hard to adapt after long years with Bash/ZSH. Yet, still I find it very nice as out-of-box solution. Two very well known plugin packages for ZSH are

  • Oh-My-ZSH
  • Prezto

Second one seems a little more light-weight though, but both are very nice. Fish has also it’s own framework:

  • Oh-My-Fish

I recommend to test various configrations and frameworks, and find one best that suits you!
